This year, Fabio Akita started a new initiative for Brazilian technology conferences. He called it [The Conf](http://www.theconf.club/). The main target was to [support Brazilian people to speak their talks in english](http://www.akitaonrails.com/2016/10/20/the-conf-initiative), so everyone in the world could listen to us. We have lot's of great stories to tell, but as we also speak in Portuguese, non Brazilian people can't see what we're doing and how these things are important.

I had the honor to present the second day opening keynote, sharing some thoughts of how developers should behave, despite of your daily coding.

The video will soon be available.
