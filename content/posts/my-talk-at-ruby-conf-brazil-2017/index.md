Last week, I attended [Ruby Conf Brazil 2017](http://eventos.locaweb.com.br/eventos-anteriores/rubyconf-2017/) and did a talk called *"How your application behaves when everything goes wrong?"*.

The purpose of this talk was to draw attention to business decisions when your software is somehow unstable due external reasons, like External Services timeout or errors, Database crashes, Network Partitions and so on.

On these scenarios, I believe most of the time your application can have a valid answer, instead of only send errors to your users. I'll do a full blog post about it later.
